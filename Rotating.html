Rotating

Blankness at first. Suddenly a whirr from underneath you ; Icons of a dead art rise from the ether, they slowly churn, distorted. Off beat, out of tune, flat, uncanny. This ruined vista becomes a hub for all those bums who seek out these dives, not a bird song to be heard just as *They* like it. The illusive and intriguing *They* made it this way. If something becomes as rotten as these walls it becomes desirable to them. *They* placed those beams of light to glare in the faces of strangers. *They* bind their belongings together with strings of text to unsecure them from ownership *They* embedded the sense of dread with the noises of machines *They* alienated themselves by the allure of science fantasy Realness and craft are reminders that in the end they are scrollers and clickers. Typing away until new text appears.
