Out The Window Residency

Hello we are Out The Window!

-By a still lake side a pearl white shell rests upon the water, its grooves fall inwards creating a vessel that suspends the figure of Venus in the air from the ground bellow. The scene is gently lit by sunlight, to the right stands an alcove of tall narrow trees that branch upwards with sharp leaves that cut into the sky. Beyond, the coastline is broken up by outcrops of land with hills rising from the water. In the distance, touching upon the horizon a flat plateau lies far out and is the furthest point of sight. Trees line its shore and a mound marks the meeting between earth and sky.

All eyes lay on the Venus and hers lie outwards past us. Her body is pale and smooth like marble, her neck is swan like, tilted to one side and her expression is melancholic. She holds herself against the elements as the sun casts a delicate shadow over her soft form, mapping the contours of her figure. Her feet rest angled on-top of the shell, one twisted behind letting the weight of her pose shift rightwards. The hair of Venus flows in a great mass in the wind, reaching down to her thigh creating a great wave of golden locks.
